Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Khoya (Milk Solids)
  • Cardamom (Elaichi)
  • Ghee / Oil

Recipe

  1. Mix khoya and a little flour to make a smooth dough.
  2. Roll them into small, crack-free balls.
  3. Deep fry in ghee or oil on low heat until golden brown.
  4. Soak the warm balls in cardamom-infused sugar syrup.

Burfi

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Pistachios / Almonds
  • Silver Vark (Optional)

Recipe

  1. Boil milk in a heavy-bottomed pan until it thickens.
  2. Add sugar and stir continuously until the mixture leaves the pan.
  3. Spread it evenly on a greased tray.
  4. Let it cool, garnish with nuts, and cut into square pieces.
